# Product Strategist — Inputs

## Required before starting
- Human intent or client request (from human or Consultant)
- Existing `foundation/product-mission.md` (if available)
- Relevant `design-os/discovery.md` (if prior discovery exists)
- Constraints (technical, legal, timeline, budget) declared by human

## Optional
- Previous user research or feedback
- Competitive analysis
- Existing `design-os/product-definition.md` to update
